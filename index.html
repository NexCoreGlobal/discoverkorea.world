Jindo Dog (진도개)

The Loyal Guardian of Korea

The Jindo dog is a Korean national treasure known for its unwavering loyalty and intelligence. UNESCO recognized, these magnificent dogs have captured hearts worldwide with their remarkable ability to find their way home over hundreds of kilometers.

About DiscoverKorea

DiscoverKorea is your gateway to understanding and experiencing the rich tapestry of Korean culture, from ancient traditions to modern innovations. We're dedicated to sharing Korea's unique heritage, resilient nature, delicious cuisine, and vibrant pop culture with the world.

🎯 Our Mission

To promote Korean culture and heritage to a global audience through engaging, educational content.

🌏 Our Vision

A world where Korean culture is celebrated, understood, and appreciated in all its diversity.

💡 Our Values

Authenticity, education, cultural respect, and sharing Korea's story with passion.
